About us

Mount Everest Secondary Boarding School (MESBS), established in 2040 B.S., is a trusted and reputed academic institution located at Siraha-7, Siraha District, Madhesh Province, Nepal. With more than four decades of experience, the school is committed to providing quality education, discipline, and holistic development in a safe and student-friendly environment.

MESBS offers Montessori Pre-School (PG–UKG), School Level (Class 1–10), and +2 programs in Management, Science, Law, and Education under the National Examinations Board (NEB). Supported by experienced and dedicated teachers, modern facilities, and balanced academic and co-curricular activities, the school focuses on academic excellence, moral values, life skills, and leadership to develop confident, responsible, and capable citizens of the future.
